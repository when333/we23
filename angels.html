Angel Investors

You probably expected names like Wintermute or Jump Crypto here.

But no — Safe Coin belongs to the average Joe.

Our Angel Investor Registration Phase ran transparently between September 12–16, with no minimum entry requirement. Anyone could participate — and every record remains publicly verifiable through the Internet Archive and our official social channels.

We designed this process to ensure fair, community-driven distribution, avoiding the typical low-liquidity launches where insiders dominate the supply across multiple wallets. Sorry, Hunt Brothers — this isn’t the 80s anymore (cornering the market).

To prevent presale bundling, we implemented an adaptive Telegram whitelist system powered by a custom-built RNG-based selection algorithm.

Here’s how it worked: our Telegram bot automatically screened for newly created accounts and rejected them outright — but even long-standing accounts weren’t automatically approved. Instead, eligibility was determined by a lottery-style randomization mechanism, where each Telegram user ID was run through a proprietary hash-based RNG formula. Only those whose IDs matched the algorithm’s random draw were granted the right to participate in the presale.

This made “bundling” virtually impossible. To game the system, someone would need to buy thousands of aged Telegram accounts and still depend entirely on luck to win even one whitelist slot. In short — no shortcuts, no insiders, no exploits.

We’ve shared more about our distribution thesis and approach on Medium.
